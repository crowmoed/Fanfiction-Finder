## ToS situation
Wattpad's ToS: users must agree they are human and not a program collecting data. Automated scraping is explicitly prohibited.

Options:
1. Accept the risk (gray area, many tools do this)
2. Slow scraping + human-like behavior to reduce detection
3. Find another data source for Wattpad-heavy fandoms

## Multi-account DB idea
Scale challenge: if DB hits 500 MB+ across all fandoms, Neon Launch tier gets expensive.

**Idea from friend:** Split fandoms across two Neon accounts — large fandoms on one, small fandoms on another.

Alternative: stay on one account, raise word floor for large fandoms to keep size manageable.
